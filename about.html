What is dabr?

  • A mobile web interface to Twitter's API
  • An open source project by @davidcarrington with inspirations from @whatleydude
  • Secure, storing your Twitter login details in an encrypted cookie on your machine, and never stored on the website

What is dabr.nl?

  • A Dutch mirror ran bij @berkes on webschuur.com
  • Is tracked by PIWIK. No data will ever be sold, given away or disclosed in another way, unless forced by Dutch law. No passwords or other sensitive data is tracked in PIWIK.
